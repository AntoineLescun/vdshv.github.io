Liposomal Technology

Unfortunately, when you ingest any vitamin, your stomach destroys more of the vitamin than it is able to absorb.

The reason for this is very simple - any ingested compound is up against powerful digestive acids that typically break down everything they come across.

So the dirty little secret that most supplement companies fail to reveal is that if you take a standard supplement in tablet or pill form 80-90% of the active ingredients will be lost in the harsh environment of the digestive system. This is where liposomes are able to help.

Liposomes are small bubbles of fat – which are called Pholosophids.

By using breakthrough technology to insert selected active ingredients into the liposomal bubbles we can ensure the ingredients are not destroyed and instead reach their target.

Because liposomes are structuredvery similar to our body cells, they can easily latch onto our cells and be carried throughout the bloodstream. This fast body-wide delivery system allows our products to achieve exceptional absorption unrivaled by any other non-invasive means.

Due to their unique properties, liposomes have been used in drug formulation for more than 20 years. Just recently liposomes have started to get attention in the food supplements market as well.
