What are liposomal encapsulation and liposomes?

In simple terms, liposomes are small bubbles of fat. Through our state-of-the-art technology selected active ingredients are carefully deployed into these liposomal bubbles. Because liposomes are structured very similar to our body cells, they can easily latch onto cells and be carried throughout the bloodstream. This fast body-wide delivery system allows us to achieve exceptional absorption unrivaled by any other non-invasive means.
