/ 01SUPPLY ?

There will be a total of 9,999 Baller Head’s NFT’s. Each Baller Head will be unique, generated by an art algorithm mixing different trait categories such as: background, skin, hat, eyes, eye accessories, eyebrows, mouth, beard, and unique themes.

/ 02MINT DATE ?

The launch date will soon be announced! Make sure you subscribe to our discord channel for updates, and register to our whitelist for the pre-sale. Find all the requirements to join the whitelist in our discord channel.

/ 03PRICE ?

The price will soon be announced! Register to our whitelist for our pre-sale. Find all the requirements to join the whitelist in our discord channel.

/ 04HOW MANY CAN I MINT ?

For the pre-sale, you can mint a maximum of 3 Baller Head’s, and the total supply for the pre-sale is TBD. For the public sale, you can mint a maximum of 5 Baller Head’s.

/ 05HOW TO MINT ?

You will be able to mint it directly through our website by clicking on the button: MINT NOW.

Yes.
