WHAT IS SHAREPOINT MIGRATION?

No matter how advanced or sophisticated, all software eventually becomes obsolete. When time comes to replace it, the task seems overwhelmingly difficult.

There is so much that needs to be transferred from the old infrastructure to the new one - data, features, user access permissions. If any of that is accidentally lost in the process, it would have to be recreated from scratch. (Not a pleasant proposition.) To sum things up: a migration is a complicated procedure with many pitfalls, and thus requires excellent planning and problem-solving skills. Thankfully Consovenio's SharePoint experts know how to do it right. We provide complete coverage of every step - from the initial analysis to post-migration support and health checks. Our SharePoint migration tools ensure that your transition is smooth and error-free - and that you walk away satisfied that your new setup works for you just as well, if not better, than the old one.

ON-PREMISES MIGRATION

This involves updating your Intranet to the newest version of the SharePoint platform. (For example, going from SharePoint 2016 to SharePoint 2019.) It works just like updating the OS on your phone. With the new version, you and your employees will gain access to the newest features and bug fixes - including SharePoint design and UI improvements that make your Intranet more user friendly. The added convenience for users is paired with benefits that managers will surely appreciate - the peace of mind that comes with better security. HYBRID SHAREPOINT ARCHITECTURE

In some use cases, SharePoint online is not a sufficient solution. The feature and workflow customization that it offers turns out to be too limited for the customer. In this case, it is best to combine the advantages of SharePoint online with the customization possibilities offered by a SharePoint on premise solution. Many of our clients have chosen this exact path - we call it the "hybrid solution." It is truly the best of both worlds - the convenience of the cloud support, plus the customization potential of an on-premise setup. Sounds too complicated? No worries - we'll make it work for you, because we've done it before a countless number of times. And we'll do it exactly the way you want it done. With Consovenio's help, you can configure your hybrid setup to your exact specifications - so that is perfect for the needs of your business.
