ABOUT US

Consovenio is a multicultural team of Microsoft developers and SharePoint Experts who come from many different countries and continents. Yet there is one thing that brings us all together: a passion for current trends in technology and business. We are always eager to learn - and to share what we've discovered with others. That is why we have teamed up on a mission to provide our knowledge and experience to organizations that struggle with their SharePoint Intranet.

We are committed to taking on any and all new clients that could benefit from SharePoint multilingual Support – even the largest global corporations. Whatever the size or status of your organization, we've likely already dealt with something similar. Throughout our 11 years of work experience, we have worked with various companies, ranging from small local businesses to international corporations. Our know-how is your golden opportunity. If you decide to work with us, you can be sure that our team of SharePoint developers will be led by an experienced SharePoint project manager, assigned specifically to match the needs of your project.

Even if you ask us to do something that seems complicated and difficult, it won't intimidate us. On our journey we have already encountered all kinds of scenarios: from the simplest tasks to the most demanding clients. There were times, for example, that we had to migrate SharePoint with many customizations into new and largely incompatible environments. We've risen to many such challenges over the years, and are ready to tackle new projects with the kind of confidence that comes from a deep mastery of our field.
